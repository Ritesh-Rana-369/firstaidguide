BASIC FIRST AID TECHNIQUES

What is First Aid

First Aid is the initial assistance or treatment provided to an injured person before professional medical care is available.

Aims of First Aid

  1. To preserve life.
  2. To prevent the worsening of the person’s condition.
  3. To promote recovery.
  4. To help ensure safe transportation to the nearest healthcare facility.

First Aid and Law

  • Good Samaritan: Refers to someone who voluntarily provides basic first aid in an emergency to an injured person.
  • Legal Protection: The Indian Good Samaritan and Bystanders Protection Guidelines offer legal protection to Good Samaritans who provide aid in emergencies.
  • Consent of the Person in Need: When administering first aid to a conscious person, their consent must be obtained before providing care. If they refuse help, stay nearby, inform authorities, and contact emergency services.
  • Respecting Privacy: Respecting personal privacy is crucial. First aiders should keep crowds away, use screens if necessary, and cover exposed body parts to maintain dignity.

How to Deal with an Emergency

  • Keep the surroundings safe.
  • Check the condition of the patient.
  • Ask for help.
  • Provide first aid as soon as possible.
